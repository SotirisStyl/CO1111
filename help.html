To play the treasure hunt, proceed to follow the steps below:

Click on "Start the hunt" and write your name in the box. If your name already exists, an input error will appear, and you will have to try with a different username.

When you submit your name, you then have to select a Treasure Hunt session from the list that will be provided to be able to view the questions.

At the top of your screen, on the question page, you will be able to see your username, score and have access to the scoreboard.

There are a lot of different type of questions, such as numerical, boolean(yes/no), text and multiple-choice. When you submit your answer a submission alert message will appear.

Some questions require to have your location turned on and also a skip button is provided to give you the ability to skip a specific question.

After you finish the quiz, the app will take you to the scoreboard page, which will display your final score and rank among all other players. Then you can either exit or click the "play again" button to return to the Treasure Hunt list page.

Thank you for becoming a member of our community.
