Information

The treasure hunt app is widely available on all smartphones and mobile devices that have a current, HTML5-compatible web browser. The application's main purpose is to persuade younger users to play individually or in groups to gain points. The online software is designed to be appealing and accessible to all users, encouraging them to play in groups. Because the Treasure Hunt is made up of several forms of questions, each user will have the opportunity to complete the entire session. Correct answers will gain points, while incorrect responses will cost points.In addition to answering the question correctly, players must be at roughly the proper area because certain questions require location access. As a consequence, scores will be shown on the scoreboard page to illustrate player rankings.

To present our app and encourage viewers to try the game, we rely primarily on social media channels such as Instagram, Twitter, and TikTok, which are the most popular. We would appreciate it if you will join our family and help us in this way. Further information about our social media platforms may be found at the top of the page.
